Quality Control Receiving Checklist (Form 13) is used to track and verify:

  • Product receiving quality and condition
  • Temperature monitoring at receiving
  • Inspection for physical hazards
  • Proper storage and transfer times
  • Quality controller verification and signature

Key Points:

  • Duration between receiving and storage should ideally be under 30 minutes
  • All products should pass physical hazard inspection
  • Temperature readings should be within acceptable ranges
  • Each entry must be signed by a quality controller
